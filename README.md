# HP-Nigeria-Sales-Dashboard-Excel
Excel dashboard analyzing sales performance, profit trends, regional contribution, and product performance across HP Nigeria sales data.
---
##  Project Objective

---

The objective of this project is to analyze HP Nigeria sales data to evaluate regional sales performance, product profitability, and overall sales trends.

This dashboard helps identify top-performing regions and products to support better business decisions.

---

## 📊 Business Questions Answered

1. Which region generates the highest sales and profit?
2. Which products contribute the most sales?
3. How do sales and profit trends change over time?
4. Which products are the most profitable?
5. How does regional performance compare?
6. Which products underperform in sales?
7. What are the monthly trends in sales and profit?
8. How do product sales vary across regions?

---

## Key Insights

- Some regions consistently outperform others in both sales and profit.
- A few products contribute heavily to total revenue.
- Sales and profit trends fluctuate over time.
- Product profitability varies significantly across categories.
- Certain products perform better in specific regions.

---

## Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products.
- Improve sales strategies in underperforming regions.
- Increase stock availability for profitable products.
- Use trend analysis to improve forecasting and planning.
- Monitor regional product performance regularly.
